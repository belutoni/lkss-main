About this year's edition
=========================

Welcome to the 2026 edition of the **Linux Kernel Summer School**! This year's edition is based
around a series of hands-on exercises and courses, meant to help the students get acquainted
with the Linux kernel development. The topics disussed in this edition are listed in :ref:`2026-schedule`.

Hardware
--------

This edition is based on the :ref:`FRDM-IMX93 <frdm-imx93-board-info>` and :ref:`LKSS-DAUGHTER-BOARD <lkss-daughter-board-info>` boards.

Getting started
---------------

Make sure you have a working development environment (see :ref:`infrastructure`) and a
:ref:`FRDM-IMX93 <frdm-imx93-board-info>` board [#]_.
